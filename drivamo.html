How It Works

Every Drive. Every Metric. Scored.

Drivamo connects directly to our simulator hardware and captures hundreds of data points per session. The AI engine processes them into structured categories — each with a score and plain-language feedback that any manager can act on immediately.

  • Automated scoring across 8 core driving skill categories
  • Sub-metric breakdown with Good / Pass / Fail ratings
  • Session history and trend tracking per driver
  • Exportable PDF reports for HR and fleet managers
  • Instant comparison across candidate pools
  • Fraud-proof — no self-reported data, only simulator truth

Post-Session Video Review

Drivers Learn by Watching Themselves

After every session, drivers receive a full recording of their drive with interactive event markers overlaid on the timeline. Each flagged moment is linked directly to the metric it affected — so drivers don't just see a score, they understand exactly why it happened.

  • Click any event marker to jump straight to that moment in the video
  • Color-coded flags: green for good technique, orange for warnings, red for fails
  • AI commentary explains what went wrong and how to correct it
  • Accessible from home on any device — drivers improve between sessions
  • Builds self-awareness and accelerates skill development without extra instructor time

Security & Compliance

Your Data Stays Yours

Built from the ground up with one principle: your data belongs to you and only you — from physical storage to cloud computing.

🔒

Own Servers + DigitalOcean EU Cloud

Drivamo runs on AAER-owned physical servers combined with DigitalOcean's EU cloud infrastructure — delivering enterprise-grade reliability, DDoS protection, and encrypted transport, while ensuring raw driver data and session recordings never leave our direct control.

EU-Based Infrastructure DigitalOcean Secured End-to-End Encrypted
🛡️

Privacy in Mind — Your Hub, Your Data

Every simulator hub operates in a fully isolated environment. Your driver data — session recordings, scores, and candidate profiles — is visible exclusively to your team. No other company or hub can ever access it. Whether used for recruitment screening or driver upskilling, your data is never shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.
